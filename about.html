ABOUT (KGSB)
KODAGU GOWDA SAMAJA® BANGALORE

Kodagu Gowda Samaja® Bangalore was first started by a handful of community members who migrated from Kodagu to Bangalore in search of Jobs. Many landed working in several Govt. Departments including DRDO, B.E.L, H.A.L, B.E.M.L, H.M.T, State Police, State Govt. Departments, Educational Institutions, Indian Defence and also in other Private Organizations. Few of them even started their own companies serving local Bangalore people and made a name for themselves.

Inception and Evolution:

In February 1965 under the guidance of Sri. Kedambadi P Mudappa, Sri. Thotambailu Belliappa, Sri. Cheriamane Ponnappa, Sri. Amme Shivaraiah, Sri. Nadubati Seetaram, Sri. kedambadi Vasudevan, Sri. Chettimada Chinappa , Sri. Nidube Chinnappa, Sri. Belliana Errappa and a few others conducted the 1st Meeting of Kodagu Gowda Samaja® Bangalore at Krishna Bhavan Restaurant, Malleshwaram. Further In the Year 1966 Sri. Kedambadi P Mudappa was made as the 1st President of the Kodagu Gowda Samaja® Bangalore and first celebrated kail Murtha Habba in Bangalore with the local Arebhase Gowda’s. People joined hands by chipping in whatever monetary help and support they could provide, including getting food arranged for the people. The Committee members used to meet at Krishna Bhavan Restaurant in Malleshwaram and later in Moti Mahal Hotel near Gandhi Nagar, Bangalore and discuss about the samaja’s up-liftment. . In the Subsequent years, Sri. Kunjila Poovaiah, Sri. Cheriamane Muthanna, Sri. Cheriamane Ganapathy, Sri. Kallambi Ganapathy, Sri. Guddemane Jayarama, Sri. Govindammana Acchaiah, Sri. Kaleyanda Machaiah, Sri. Poojari Monappa joined the team and worked towards the samaja development. This laid the foundation for forming a Community Base which eventually led to the formation of our own Samaja in Kaval Byrasandra, RT Nagar.

Infrastructure Development:

Sri. Padichettira Mandanna, then president of KGSB and also ex-SBI Officers Association President, was one of the main active samaja member who was responsible for purchasing the Kaval Byrasandra, RT Nagar Revenue site for the samaja and started the construction of the building. But Unfortunately he expired in the middle of the construction period and this was later completed by Sri. Thotambailu Belliappa. This helped the community people meet each other often on various festival occasions, upliftment of our kodagu arebhase gowda community in Bangalore and also to help the community students who came to Bangalore for higher education.

Ullala Facility: A New Chapter:

Later with the community base increasing over the years, Sri. Thotambailu Belliappa and Sri. Ammavana Vasu, visualized of having a bigger samaja building, approached the Bangalore Development Authority ( BDA ) and Sri. Kombarana G Bopaiah, then Hon’ble Speaker, Govt. of Karnataka. With the help of Sri. K G Bopaiah and then Karnataka Chief Minister Sri. B S Yedurappa, a C.A Site No 6, in vishweshwaraiah layout 5th block, Ullala, measuring around 3227 Sq.Meter ( Nearly 1 Acre Plot ) was allotted to the samaja.
The Foundation laying ceremony was held on dated : 23rd February 2013 under the leadership of Sri. Ammavana Vasu, then President, Kodagu Gowda Samaja® Bangalore. Sri. K.G. Bopaiah, Hon’ble Speaker, Govt. of Karnataka and Sri. R. Ashok, Deputy Chief Minister, Govt. of Karnataka graced the occasion as chief Guests.

50th Year Golden Jubilee Celebration:

On 03rd November 2019, under the presidentship of Sri. Amme Sitaram, Kodagu Gowda Samaja® Bangalore celebrated its 50th Year Golden Jubilee Function at our Ullala Facility. This was graced by Sri Sri Sri Dr. Nirmalanandanatha Mahaswamiji, the 72nd Pontiff of Sri Adichunchanagiri Mahasamsthana Math and Sri. D.V Sadanada Gowda, Sri. K.G Bopaiah.
Today the building houses 2 Community Halls which can house around 1000+ Person moving capacity Functions with fully furnished Guest Rooms, Beautiful Lawn and a Parking lot. It also has a Kitchen and a Dining Hall which can seat around 350 Persons at a time.

Current Facilities and Leadership:

Sri. Kekada Nanaiah, Retd. DySP is the current President of Kodagu Gowda Samaja® Bangalore and is actively involved in completing the current samaja building at Ullala and also in conducting various community related programmes.

Community Engagement:

All our community festivals and family get together take place at the samaja. Further the samaja also helps in giving the Community Halls at affordable prices to others for Engagement, Marriage, Beegara Oota, Thithi Functions, Birthday parties and Family / Community Get Together.

Festivals Celebrated at Kodagu Gowda Samaja Bangalore

At Kodagu Gowda Samaja® Bangalore, Huttari, Aati and Kailmurtha is celebrated with great Pomp and show. Special Dignitaries and Guests are invited to the samaja at our Ullala, Bangalore Facility and community achievers are honoured. Solo/Group Dance Programs, Singing competitions and Cultural dances are conducted. This is followed by a sumptuous meal and followed by Valga Dance ( Coorg famous music/dance ).

Vidya Yojane

Team KGSB lead by Sri. Thumthajira Sandeep, Sri. Govindamana Praveen provide career counselling and help & guide our Arebhase Community Members in their professional and educational front. Several of the community members have got jobs through the Career Counselling group. The Team has also counselled 10th Std. and 12th Std. Students from Cauvery Junior College, Bagamandala and Aruna High School, Cherambane. Further it has also provided monetary help to needy students towards their education fees & Donated Computers and UPS Systems.
